Community Service

Howell TSA raises funds for the Relay for Life, which works to raise funds for the American Cancer Society.

Raising Money for The Cure

Together, with the various Relay for Life teams across Howell High School, we raised over 120 thousand dollars collectively for cancer research.

National TSA promotes the American Cancer Society, and it is their official National Service Project.

The American Cancer Society promotes research, education, patient services, and advocacy for those suffering from cancer.

Get Involved

To get involved, or join the TSA Relay for Life team, come see Ms. Roses in B109. Participation in other TSA Fundraising events is also an effective way to contribute, as surplus funds at the end of the year from our budget goes to the American Cancer Society.

So many people are effected by cancer, whether it's a loved one or themselves, and by contributing you can feel good knowing you made a difference.
