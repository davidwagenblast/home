Leadership Opportunities

Howell High TSA has a board consisting of 8 student members, and any interetsted student can run for office in the Fall.

Get Started

In order to run for a TSA office, it is suggested you get an understanding of how TSA as an Organization works, and how it works on the chapter level.

To be eligible to run, you need to be a member of TSA. All students who've taken a tech class are eligible for membership.

After filling out a permission slip from B109, you will be ready to run. Fill out an officer application, and now you just have to get the votes!




Positions

With eight different positions, you can use your interests and pick the best position for your skill set.

President

"The duties vested in me by my office are to preside at regular and special meetings of this organization and to promote the cooperation in carrying out the activities and work of our organization."

Vice-President

"It is the duty of my office to see that we always have a strong membership, a good work program, and are alert to the welfare of our chapter."

Secretary

"It is my responsibility to see that accurate and proper records are kept of all business and correspondence of this association."

Treasurer

"It is the duty of my office to keep accurate records of all funds and to see that our financial obligations are met promptly."

Parlimentarian

"TIt is the duty of my officer to guarantee that Roberts Rules of Order are followed at all special meetings."

Reporter

"It is the duty to see that our school, community, state, and national association have a complete report of our organization's activities."

Historian

"It is my responsibility to see that all activities by the organization are logged and recorded."

Seargent-At-Arms

"It is my responsibility to see that the assembly is comfortable and properly welcomed. It is also my duty to serve as doorkeeper for this organization."
